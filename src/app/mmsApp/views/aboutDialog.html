Metamorphosys Tech Demo Dev.0.2.0.

The Metamorphosys tools are used for the design and analysis of electronics. In this tech demo, you'll be able to quickly generate a circuit board design for an Ara Module with a 1x2 form-factor.

Tutorial exercise:

Grab a sensor component from the SENSORS folder and drag it onto the canvas.

As an alternate method, you can also right-click on the chosen component and select "Add to design" from the context-menu.

Connect the sensor's power ports to the corresponding interfaces of the Power System. Then connect its communication interface to the corresponding interface on the GP ASIC.

Click the play button to automatically generate a PCB (printed circuit board). This can take a few minutes.

After reviewing the results, iterate on your design by making changes, adding more components, and generating new PCBs.

A Note About the Tech Demo:

This is a limited prototype aimed at showing the new editing, collaboration, and cloud-based computation capabilities of our upcoming web version. Check out the metamorphosys-desktop project to use the full capabilities of the Metamorphosys tools, including:

  • analog and digital behavior simulation
  • export-for-manufacturing
  • app-to-firmware comms prototyping
  • parts cost estimation
  • much larger component library (thousands of components)
