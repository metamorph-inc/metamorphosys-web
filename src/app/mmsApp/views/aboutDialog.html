Metamorphosys Tech Demo Dev.0.1.0.

The Metamorphosys tools are used for the design and analysis of electronics, and have special features for designing modules for Google's Project Ara. In this tech demo, you'll be able to quickly generate a circuit board design for an Ara Module with a 1x2 form-factor.

What To Do

Grab a sensor component from the SENSORS folder and drag it onto the canvas.

Connect the sensor's power ports to the corresponding interfaces of the Power System. Then connect its communication interface to the corresponding interface on the GP ASIC.

Click the play button to automatically generate a PCB (printed circuit board)

After reviewing the results, iterate on your design by making changes, adding more components, and generating new PCBs.
